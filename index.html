iOS App

SplintFab Scanner

A professional 3D scanning app for orthotics and prosthetics clinicians. SplintFab Scanner turns an iPhone's front TrueDepth camera into a precise 3D measurement tool for capturing feet, ankles, and hands.

  • Real-time, on-device 3D capture — no cloud, no account
  • Exports an open PLY point cloud for lab fabrication
  • Built for iPhone X and later, iOS 16+
