SplintFab Scanner

Professional 3D scanning for orthotics and prosthetics — powered by the iPhone TrueDepth camera, processed entirely on device.

SplintFab Scanner is an iOS app built by Boom Consulting LLC for clinicians who fabricate custom orthotics and prosthetics. It turns an iPhone's front TrueDepth camera into a precise 3D measurement tool, capturing a coloured 3D model of a patient's foot, ankle, or hand in seconds — with no cloud, no account, and no data leaving the device.

Why clinicians use it

📶

Precise depth capture

Uses Apple's TrueDepth sensor and real-time 3D reconstruction to build an accurate, coloured point cloud of the scanned body part.

🔒

Private by design

Every scan is processed and stored on the device. Nothing is uploaded, and the app contains no analytics or third-party tracking.

💾

Open export format

Scans export as a standard OBJ mesh, ready for the fabrication lab and common CAD tools.

⚡

Fast in-clinic workflow

Capture, review, and share a scan in under a minute — no scanning rig or dedicated hardware required.

How it works

Scan

Hold the iPhone 25–35 cm from the foot, ankle, or hand and move slowly around it. The app aligns each depth frame into a single 3D model in real time.

Review & save

Check the captured model for coverage, then tap Save. Scans are kept in the app's private sandbox on your device until you delete them.

Share to the lab

Tap Share to send the scan by AirDrop, Files, or email as an OBJ mesh, ready for the fabrication lab.

Requirements

DeviceiPhone with front TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later)
Operating systemiOS 16 or later
PermissionsCamera access (required for scanning)
Export formatOBJ mesh

Patient data stays with you. SplintFab Scanner transmits nothing over the internet. A scan leaves your device only when you explicitly share it. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
